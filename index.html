About Qriosity



  • Aim – We seek to provide school students a platform to build conceptual knowledge, apply skills and achieve. Knowledge is a beautiful thing that can fill one with happiness. We at Quark promote “Education for All”.


  • Who we are? - Qriosity is a national level Aptitude test organized by the student fraternity of BITS PILANI K.K. Birla Goa Campus. Started in 2014, with a participation of 500 students, we have expanded our reach to cities all across India, getting an overwhelming turnout of 3000+ Students last year.


  • Qriosity Structure - This year qriosity is being conducted in a completely online mode for the first time since it’s conception.
    Qriosity is conducted on three levels from this year onwards:
    Level-1 for Classes 05 to 07
    Level-2 for Classes 08 to 10
    Level-3 for Classes 11 and 12
    It involves no syllabus or general knowledge and relies solely on the student's logical and analytical thinking abilities. The question paper will have various sections like maths, puzzles, code crunchers etc. Each section has its own marking scheme. The exam is generally of 2 hour length .These questions are designed in a way to properly check the intellect and problem solving abilities of the participants
